# Hanstholm 🌊

A glance at your wrist tells you whether it's worth driving to the harbour.

Wave height, period, and wind — live from the weather station at Hanstholm Havn, Nordjylland 🇩🇰. On your watch, and on your watch face as a complication. A companion app on your iPhone shows the same conditions, with its own Lock Screen widget.

Built for watchOS 10 ⌚️ and iOS 📱
